★ Bottom Line
How to trade credit spreads off the trigger box
  • Two approaches by risk tolerance. The ±100% ATR strike has 97–99% historical win rates (safer, less premium). The ±61.8% strike has 88–93% (more premium, worse loss ratio). A 30-minute hold is the minimum useful filter.
  • Consider the opposing 38.2% as a stop level. If price reaches it, the thesis may be weakening. Remember: the loss on one bad trade can equal many winners. Size accordingly.
  • Hold time appears more important than box position. Top vs bottom half: 1–4% difference. The 30-minute hold: 10–15% difference. Time in the box is the stronger signal.
  • This is a selective, infrequent setup — appears roughly 1 in 10 days. Higher historical win rates, but fewer opportunities.
