★ Bottom Line
How to use the Bilbo
  • Check the 1-hour Phase Oscillator before entering any Golden Gate. If it confirms direction (high+rising for calls, low+falling for puts), you have a 78–90% setup. If it doesn't, you have a coin flip.
  • Consider letting Bilbo winners run. A bearish Bilbo has 66% odds of reaching the full ATR and 44% of hitting 123.6%. Trail your stop instead of taking profit at 61.8%.
  • Ignore the 10-minute PO for this. It gives 5–12x less edge. Only the 60-minute timeframe captures the sustained momentum that drives Golden Gate completions.
  • Counter-trend Golden Gates are the worst trade. Bull GG with PO mid+falling: 51.5%. Bear GG with PO mid+rising: 54.2%. These setups have below-average odds.
