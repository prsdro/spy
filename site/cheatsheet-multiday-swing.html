★ Bottom Line
Key takeaways across timeframes
  • Multi-day bearish Bilbo (daily PO low+falling) = 94% completion day 1 — strongest signal across all studies (n=54). 72% reach full weekly ATR on day 1.
  • Swing GGs take weeks to develop. Day 1 completion is just 11% bull / 36% bear. Be patient or size smaller.
  • Bearish moves are faster than bullish at EVERY timeframe. Day: 65% vs 63%. Multi-day: 72% vs 65%. Swing: 36% vs 11%.
  • The higher the timeframe PO, the less predictive edge it adds. 1h PO for day mode, daily PO for multi-day, weekly PO adds minimal edge for swing.
