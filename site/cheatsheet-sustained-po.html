★ Bottom Line
How to trade sustained-PO mornings
  • By 11am the regime is set, not the entry. 89% reach 61.8% and 78% reach 78.6% — most of the obvious upside is already in the tape. Don't chase here.
  • Don't fade the PO downtick. 59% of days make a new high after PO leaves distribution. The mean-reversion of PO is normal, not a reversal signal.
  • Use the 10m fast cloud after 11am as your runner filter. Cloud stays bullish → bias close-on-high (88% close higher). Cloud flips → that flip itself runs another −0.11% to close on average. +0.82% total edge between the two regimes.
  • 30%+ of day highs print in the final 30 minutes. If the cloud is still bullish into the 3pm hour, hold for the close.
