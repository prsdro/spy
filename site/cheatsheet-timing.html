★ Bottom Line
When to trade the Golden Gate
  • Open triggers are the highest conviction trade of the day. When the GG fires at the open (gap through 38.2%), it completes 86–88% of the time. Most complete within the first hour. This is your A+ setup.
  • Morning triggers (09:00–10:00) are still good. 57–64% completion with plenty of time for the move to develop. These are solid B-grade setups.
  • Afternoon triggers are coin flips. By 13:00–14:00, completion is 39–56%. The day is running out. Size down or skip.
  • 15:00 bullish triggers rarely complete. Only 16% completion — there's no time left. But bearish 15:00 triggers still complete 30% because selling cascades are fast.
  • Bearish is faster than bullish at every hour. Bear GGs complete more often and more quickly. Selling pressure carries further than buying pressure — plan your bear plays more aggressively.
