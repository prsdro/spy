01 — What Is the Trigger Box
The zone between PDC and the trigger level
Bearish box: open below PDC but above the put trigger (−23.6%). Happens 22.6% of all days (1,462 days).
Bullish box: open above PDC but below the call trigger (+23.6%). Happens 26.3% of all days (1,698 days).

  • Bull → Bear reversal: 72.7% reach put trigger, 49.6% reach bear GG (n=935)

★ Bottom Line
How to trade the trigger box
  • Days that hold the trigger box for 1 hour see a GG open 80%+ of the time. Note: most of those GGs trigger during the first hour itself (70%). The hold time is a quality filter on the full day’s outcome, not a “what happens next” prediction.
  • Longer hold = higher quality filter. 30 min filter = ~72% GG rate, 1 hour = ~80%. Each 30 minutes of holding is associated with ~10 more percentage points.
  • When PDC is reclaimed in the first hour, the reversal tends to have legs — 73% reach the opposite trigger historically.
  • If GG hasn’t triggered after 1 hour of holding, there’s still a 55–60% historical chance it triggers later. Below 50% by ~2.5 hours. Effectively over by 5 hours (2:30 PM).
